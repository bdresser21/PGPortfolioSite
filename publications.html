Dr Patrick Goold is an expert in intellectual property law, private law, and legal theory. He researches the history and philosophy of intellectual property law.

Book

Goold, P. IP Accidents: Accidental Infringement of Intellectual Property and What To Do About It. Cambridge University Press.
